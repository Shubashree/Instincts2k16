24 hour Theatre

It's that time of the year again,Instincts is back in town. SSN's Lights Out Please takes this opportunity to bring to you "24 Hour Theatre". Do you have what it takes to think on your feet and direct? Think you've got what it takes to improvise and add your own creative twist to any quirky character? This is an opportunity for all theatre enthusiasts and aspiring actors to meet up and show off their creativity and talent in putting together and executing a short play within 24 hours. So what are you waiting for? Head over, grab a script and act away!

Rapid Fire Art

Do you think you can dazzle us with your artistic skill in any medium? Then Rapid Fire Art event is your biggest chance to portray that you are master of them all. This event will require the participant to unleash his/her talent in the select medium provided on the spot.

Rules:

• Teams of 3-4
• Total of three rounds
• Duration of each round: 30mins
• Cumulative scores of the three rounds will be used to choose the winner
• Materials will be provided.

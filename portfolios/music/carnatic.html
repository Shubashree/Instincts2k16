CARNATIC MUSIC EVENT (SOLO) – SWARALAYA (11/3/2016, FRIDAY)

SSN participation allowed The Margazhi music season isn’t over yet. See yourself in love with classical music? We give you the stage to showcase your Kutcheri skills. Come, show us your talent in the Classical music event “Swaralaya”.

Event Details

This is a two stage, classical music solo event. Participants will be judged strictly based on their mettle in classical music, Carnatic or Hindustani. Participants may be either instrumentalists (non-percussion) or vocalists.

Rules

Prelims
1. In this stage, each participant will be given four minutes to perform any classical piece of her/his choice.
2. Use of Shruthi box is permitted, use of accompaniments isn’t.
3. 6 to 8 participants will be selected for the second/final round as per the judge’s discretion.
Finals
1. Each finalist will have seven minutes on stage.
2. The contestant may begin with a piece of her/his choice. However, the judge may feel free to interrupt the contestant.
3. The contestant may be asked to answer questions or perform pieces as per the request of the judge and a holistic assessment of the contestant will be made by the judge based on the contestant’s response and performance.
4. Cash prizes will be awarded to the top three contestants as chosen by the judge.
Note: Those participating in the event are requested to send a mail to thessnmusicband@gmail.com informing the name of the participant, college and contact details.

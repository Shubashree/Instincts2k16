LIGHT MUSIC SOLO VOCAL – SSN IDOL(11/3/2016, FRIDAY)

Looking to prove your mettle as a singer? You’re at the right place. Flaunt your singing skills in the light music solo vocal event. Enthral us with your voice, all eyes are going to be on you and the stage is all yours. The event is not to be missed for all you aspiring singers!

Event Details

1. Light music vocal solo event.
2. Participants MUST be college students with a valid college ID card.
3. This is a two stage event: Prelims and Finals.
4. 8-10 participants will be chosen for the Finals by the judge. The judge’s decision is final.

Rules

Songs performed by participants must be in Indian languages. Presence of a considerable amount of foreign language in a performance might lead to disqualification and this is up to the judge.
Registration for this event is on-spot. Registration will close one hour prior to the beginning of the event (check schedule for event timing).
Prelims
1. Each participant will be given 3 minutes with set up.
2. Use of karaoke backing track is allowed. Participants using backing tracks must submit their tracks (in a USB device) at the desk before the event begins, aux connections will be available too. Instrumental accompaniment is not allowed.
3. Use of Shruti box is permitted; use of metronome isn’t.
4. The judge’s decision will be final.
Finals
1. The participant will be given 9 minutes to perform two songs; variety in genre of songs performed will fetch more points.
2. One instrumental accompaniment may be used.
3. Rest of the rules are the same as in the prelims round.
4. As always, the judge’s decision will be final.
REGISTRATION will be done on the spot

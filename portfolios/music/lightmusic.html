LIGHT MUSIC BAND EVENT- DHWANI

Think you could be the next Avial, Agam or Indian Ocean? Dhwani, the band event of Instincts is back for all you enthusiasts. Come battle it out with people who think likewise and prove your talent. Music lovers, this event is handcrafted for you guys

Event Details

1. Light Music Band event.
2. Participants must be college students with Valid ID cards. Inter college teams are not allowed.
3. This is a single stage event.

Rules

1. Bands must consist of a minimum of 4 participants and a maximum of 10 participants with at least, one vocalist and two instrumentalists.
2. Songs performed must be in Indian languages only.
3. The time given for each band performance is 10 minutes including the time for set up of instruments and sound check.
4. Versatility in genres of songs performed and own compositions will fetch additional points.
5. A standard five piece drum kit and a keyboard stand will be provided. Participants are requested to bring other instruments.
6. No Participant can be a part of more than one band.

Registration

Bands are requested to pre-register for the event by sending their profile to thessnmusicband@gmail.com along with a link (YouTube, Dropbox, Drive) to a performance by the band (10 minutes). The deadline for the submission is 11:59pm on the 8th of March 2016 (Tuesday). The mail must contain the names of all band members, name of the college and contact details of one member for correspondences. Once selected, the band will be informed about their slots on event day.

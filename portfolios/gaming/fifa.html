Rules

1. The fixtures will be formed at random. Half-time length is 4 minutes.
2. Two players will be selected for a match.
3. They have the choice of using either a keyboard or a joystick.
4. Teams will be picked by the players according to their choice. Fair play should be followed here.
5. Any other Custom tactics used will lead to disqualification.
6. The decision of the organizers will be final.

Other rules and regulations:

Stadium is decided between players, otherwise it is neutral.
Participants must bring their own joysticks.
Participants may set their own controls in the configuration window.
Default settings for camera and other in-game settings.
In case of a draw after 90 minutes, the player with more shots on target wins.
If players have equal shots on target, then possession will be taken into consideration.
Any connections errors or interruptions will result in a single half rematch.

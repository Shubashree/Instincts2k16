Rules

1) Games will be held in one-on-one team fashion.
2) Winners will proceed to the next round.
3) Re-entry is permitted.
4) Inter-college teams are allowed.
5) All rounds will be conducted in a knockout fashion.
6) Teams can consist of 3 to 5 players.
7) Choice of maps will be done by the organizers.
8) Contestants can bring their own gaming gear, but the organizers are not responsible if they are not compatible.
9) Swearing or abusing the opponents will lead to DISQUALIFICATION.
10) All decisions by the organizers and moderators are binding and final.
11) The above rules may be changed without any further notice.

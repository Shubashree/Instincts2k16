MASH IT UP

If you think you are spontaneous, witty, one with the creative head who is looking for fun then “MASH IT UP “ is your floor. This game consists of two levels. The prelims round is AV visual round where a video sequence from a movie or any clipping is played and then the audio of the same visual is played again. You have all the freedom to innovate and reinvent the video and perform. The finals is the legendary “Channel Surfing” where a series of channels is surfed ranging from Animal Planet to Shankara and all you need to do is to be spontaneous, creative and of course funny and mimic the channels as they are surfed. So roll up your sleeves!! Get your creative caps ON!!! Let your imaginations run wild!!!! SO ARE YOU READY MASH IT UP????!!!

Rules:

1) 3-4 member per team
2)Prelims consists of AV round two min each for video observation,concept discussion and performance
3) No abusive languages.
4) a team should represent one college only
5) id card is must
6)the final judgment will be based on innovation, performance, humour, creativity, timing.
7) decisions of judges is final
8)gadgets not allowed during performance
9)the selected teams move to finals- channel surfing
10) no separate time for preparation as the judges surf the channels the team can perform.
11)Max 3-5 minutes performance.

LAWYER UP

Doing its debut this year, this event is one for all the legal minds. You think you are better than Perry Mason? Prove it by defending your client !

Rules

1. This is an individual event.
2. The event consists of two rounds. There will be a preliminary round from which participants will be screened to participate in the final round.
3. Each participant will be given a worded case and he/she will be judges on their ability to represent their client.
4. Judges may also impose their questions at the end of each argument.
5. Use of any unparlimentary language will lead to immediate disqualification.
6. Knowledge of exact laws/sections is not a necessity. Your argument may be logical/rational supported with legal consistency.
7. Preparation time will be given for prelims and finals accordingly.

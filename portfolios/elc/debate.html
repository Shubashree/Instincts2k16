SSN Debates

SSN Debates is our flagship event and has evolved into one of the most popular parliamentary style debates in the city. This event is open to teams of two. Cross college teams are allowed.

Rules

1. Two members per team
2. In each round, two teams will be pitted against each other.
3. Points of information allowed, the details of which will be put up in a dedicated post shortly.
4. Topics will be out soon! The list of topics and study guidelines will be given in advance for the prelims.

Speech Rules:

Prelims:
1. Each speaker’s substantive speech time is 5 minutes.
2. Rebuttal time for each team is 3 minutes, where either speaker from each team can choose to do the rebuttal
3. Questioning by the judges and audience will roughly be allotted 5 minutes per team.
Finals:
1. Each speaker’s substantive speech time is 7 minutes.
2. Rebuttal time for each team is 4 minutes, where either speaker from each team can choose to do the rebuttal
3. Questioning by the judges and audience will roughly be allotted 5 minutes per team.
